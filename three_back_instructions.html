Three-Back Test Instructions Page

For this test, you will be initially be shown three pre-trial letters. For each subsequent letter, you will have to decide whether or not the letter appeared three letters previously. There will be 40 trials.

Example:

Pre-Trial 1: A

Pre-Trial 2: B

Pre-Trial 3: C

Trial 1: D ; the answer will be "no match"

Trial 2: B ; the answer will be "match"

Trial 3: C ; the answer will be "match"
