Test Instructions Page

This is a research study in which we are looking to see if we can predict your performance on cognitive working memory tasks based on your browsing patterns.

For this test, you will be initially be shown two pre-trial letters. For each subsequent letter, you will have to decide whether or not the letter appeared two letters previously. There will be 40 trials.

Example:

Pre-Trial 1: A

Pre-Trial 2: B

Trial 1: C ; the answer will be "no match"

Trial 2: B ; the answer will be "match"

Trial 3: C ; the answer will be "match"
